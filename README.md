# 🧶 GONGADI

GONGADI is a modern e-commerce platform designed to promote and sell authentic Indian handicrafts, handmade products, traditional arts, and cultural heritage items. The platform connects artisans directly with customers, helping preserve India's rich craftsmanship while providing a seamless online shopping experience.

---

## ✨ Project Overview

The goal of GONGADI is to create a digital marketplace where artisans from different states of India can showcase and sell their handcrafted products such as:

- 🧶 Gongadi Wool Blankets
- 🏺 Pottery
- 🎨 Handmade Paintings
- 🧺 Bamboo Crafts
- 🪵 Wooden Handicrafts
- 👗 Handloom Sarees
- 🪔 Brass & Metal Crafts
- 🧵 Embroidery & Textile Products
- 🧺 Tribal Arts
- and many more...
